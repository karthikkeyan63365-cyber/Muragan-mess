Our Story

Made with Love,
Served with Pride

Murugan Mess was started in 1995 by Mr. Murugan and his family in T. Nagar, Chennai. What began as a small 10-table mess has grown into one of the most loved spots in the neighbourhood — serving the same authentic recipes, the same flavours, the same love — every single day.

No shortcuts. No frozen food. Every dish is freshly prepared each morning using locally sourced vegetables and traditional spices. That's our promise to you.
